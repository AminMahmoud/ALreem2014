Our Services

AlreemSafety being a fully licensed and approved Fire Protection Contractor undertake the design, supply, installation, testing, commissioning, certification and preventive maintenance of the entire range of Fire Alarm, Fire Fighting and Emergency Evacuation Systems. We have a dedicated team of Technical Managers, Sales Officers, Service Engineers and Technicians to ensure highest quality service to our customers. Our services include the following

 

Supply, Installation, Maintenance & Certification of:

 

  • Addressable and Conventional Fire Alarm System
  • Voice Evacuation System
  • Central Battery, Emergency and Exit Lighting Systems
  • Water Based Sprinkler Systems
  • Fire Pumps
  • Fire Hose Reels/ Wet/ Dry Riser Systems
  • FM 200 System
  • Deluge System
  • Fire Extinguishing Systems
  • Fire Protection Coating Systems

We also provide 24 Hours Emergency service 365 days a year, with a guaranteed response time, to ensure your safety, through a trouble free system operation.
